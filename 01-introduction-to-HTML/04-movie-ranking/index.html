Best movies according to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all time

Spirited Away

"Spirited Away" is an enchanting tale of a young girl navigating a magical world to rescue her parents, discovering courage and friendship along the way.

A Silent Voice

"A Silent Voice" is a poignant anime about a former bully seeking redemption by reconnecting with the deaf girl he once tormented.

Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" is an epic conclusion where the Avengers assemble for a final battle to reverse Thanos's devastating snap and restore balance to the universe.
